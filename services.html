What We Offer

Motion by Mike offers a range of dance classes and services for individuals, families and organisations in Gaborone.

Group Classes

Weekly group sessions in Afrobeats, Hip Hop and Contemporary. Open to teens and adults of all skill levels.

Price: P150 per month

Private Coaching

One-on-one sessions with Mike. Great if you want to improve quickly or prepare for a specific event.

Price: P350 per hour

Kids Programme

Dance classes for children aged 5 to 14. Fun and structured lessons that build confidence.

Price: P120 per month

Corporate Workshops

Team building dance sessions for companies. Custom packages available on request.

Price: Custom quote
