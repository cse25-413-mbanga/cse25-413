Meet Mike

Mike is a professional dancer and instructor with over 15 years of experience in Afrobeats, Hip Hop and Contemporary dance. He has performed across Botswana and internationally.

Mike founded Motion by Mike in 2017 to bring quality dance education to Gaborone and build a strong local dance community.

His classes are energetic, welcoming and open to all levels — whether you have never danced before or want to take your skills further.

Experience & Achievements

  • 15+ years of professional dance experience
  • Over 500 students trained
  • Performed at national and international events
  • Specialises in Afrobeats, Hip Hop and Contemporary
  • Youth and adult programmes available
